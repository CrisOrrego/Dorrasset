We’ve all heard the saying that “most active managers cannot beat the S&P 500 index and thus investors would be better off with a passive strategy”. While there is great truth to this it’s because the active asset management industry is poorly trained in risk management.
Investors should be aware, however, that passive strategies are also dangerous. The S&P 500 has had historical drawdowns as much as -83%.
Proper active management with expert risk management is a far superior choice.
